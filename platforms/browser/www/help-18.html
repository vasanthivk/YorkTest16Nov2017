This is your start page. Tap one of the logo tiles to let FoodAdvisr know where you are comparing. To select grocery retail or restaurant simply use the toggle at the top of the screen.

Tap on the tile for your selected location - this takes you the scanning function in your selected store if you are in grocery mode, where you will be able to scan branded and 'own brand' products; or to your selected restaurant's menu if you are in restaurant mode.
