ALLERGIES

Simply toggle the allergen choices to set your food allergies which will be used on every scan you do. If you see the black A square, the food contains allergens that are not safe for you!

Start intro again

‘MAY CONTAIN’ ALLERGIES

If you see the ‘May Contain’ sign, it means that there is a possibility that the food scanned or browsed could contain allergens that you have selected as a problem for you. We’d recommend that you avoid eating these foods.

Start intro again

Intolerances and dislikes

If you see the exclamation mark it means that the food you selected contains something you toggled in the ‘Intolerances’ list. You may want to avoid eating this food.

Start intro again

Profiles List

FoodAdvisr not only lets you set up your allergies and intolerances, but also allows you to select pre-built health profiles, which have certain ingredients automatically selected for exclusion, in line with current advice on food that may affect that condition. These profiles are built by our highly qualified and experienced nutritionists, but you should still check that the profile you select is right for you. Note: your allergy settings are MERGED with the pre-built profiles, so you can be sure that your are eating safely.

Start intro again

Communities

Many of our users have specific needs, and may be members of groups that reflect their lifestyle – this could be health issues such as Diabetes or Heart, or fitness such as your sport or activity hobby. You can select membership of a range of communities to belong to, which allows you to share your good news stories about your food scans, and also see the findings and recommendations of others in your chosen Communities.
